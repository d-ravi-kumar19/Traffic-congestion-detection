About the Project

This project was developed collaboratively by a team under the guidance of the Centre of Excellence AIML at Osmania University.

Our project aims to utilize advanced machine learning models to provide predictive insights for traffic congestion management. The solution leverages state-of-the-art neural network architectures and real-time data processing techniques to deliver accurate, actionable results.

With a focus on real-world applicability, the team designed and implemented this project to address key challenges in urban traffic monitoring and prediction. Each team member contributed uniquely, covering aspects such as model development, data processing, and deployment.

Guided by the resources and mentorship from the Centre of Excellence AIML, this project highlights our commitment to impactful AI solutions and collaborative learning.
